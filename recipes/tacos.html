Tacos

My most basic meal for the hungry boy

Ingredients

  • soft tortilla shells
  • ground beef
  • taco seasoning
  • sourcream
  • avocado

Instructions

  1. In pan cook ground beef until browned
  2. Add taco seasoning to pan with 1/3 cup water and let simmer until thickened
  3. fill shells with cooked ground beef
  4. top with sourcream and sliced avocado
